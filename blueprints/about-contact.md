# Blueprint — About + Contact

Two simple pages, one blueprint.

## About — `/about` (static)
**Goal:** credibility + who we are. Low keyword priority (brand/navigational).

### Sections
1. **Hero** — what Stockifi is, in one clear line (food-cost platform for hospitality).
2. **The why** — the problem the company exists to solve (margin leakage operators can't see). Keep honest, not grandiose.
3. **Proof** — 500+ restaurants · active markets (NO, SE, DK, DE, NL, FR, UK, BE, AT, CH, HR) · known clients.
4. **Approach / what we believe** — automation-first, food-cost-focused, integrates rather than replaces.
5. **CTA** — Book a demo.

> Avoid hero/origin-story clichés and the prohibited patterns. Plain and credible.

---

## Contact — `/contact` (static, form)
**This is the site's primary conversion target** ("Book a demo" CTA points here).

### Sections
1. **Hero** — "Book a demo" / get in touch, one line on what they'll get from the call.
2. **The form** — book-a-demo form (name, company, email, etc.). This is the conversion. Wire to the same backend as the LP form (HubSpot) — confirm in build.
3. **What happens next** — short reassurance (a real person, quick scheduling, no hard sell).
4. **Guarantee reinforcement** — slim line on the risk-free onboarding guarantee near the form.
5. **Alt contact** — email / other channel.

## Metadata
Brand/navigational titles + descriptions. Low SEO priority; conversion priority.

## Internal links out
Pricing · product pages · home.

## Notes
- Contact form is the demo conversion path; the `/intro` ad LPs are separate and not linked.
- Confirm in build whether to reuse the LP's HubSpot form wiring.
