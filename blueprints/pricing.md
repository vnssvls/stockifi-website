- **Blocks:** H1 · one line that pricing depends on setup + size · CTA.

### 2. The three plans
- **Blocks:** 3 tier cards: **Automated** · **Full Control** (★ most popular) · **Custom**. Each = what's included (per the product feature set) + CTA.
- **Copy intent:** Automated = stock count + invoice processing + 30+ accounting integrations; Full Control adds recipe cost calc, variance/wastage/profit reports, 40+ POS integrations; Custom adds multichannel, multi-location org, custom integrations, QBR.

### 3. The guarantee — risk-reversal (prominent here)
- **Blocks:** the €3,000 / 30-day risk-free onboarding guarantee, framed as confidence. This is where it gets the spotlight.
- **Copy intent:** "if we don't uncover at least €3,000 in hidden losses in 30 days, you pay nothing." Risk-free onboarding, NOT performance pricing.

### 4. How pricing works — FAQ
- **Blocks:** 4-5 Q&A: what determines price, the range (€150–400/mo, only if asked), success-based vs guarantee clarification, what's included, contract terms.

### 5. ROI nudge

### 6. CTA band
