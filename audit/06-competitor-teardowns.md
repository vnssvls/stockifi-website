# Competitor Site Teardowns + Synthesis

> Workstream A/C input. Content + narrative + on-page keyword teardown of 5 competitors, then a cross-analysis to guide Stockifi's IA and homepage structure.
> Scope note: this covers story/structure/messaging. Visual/layout feel is best read from V's screenshots. Researched 2026-05-30.

---

## Per-competitor summary

### Apicbase (BE) — outcome-led, AI-forward
- **Nav:** Solutions (8 outcome pillars) · AI Native · Platform (Develop & Control / Manage / Plan / Connect) · Customers · Pricing hidden.
- **Homepage arc:** Hero (AI in back-of-house) → 8 solution pillars → platform by operational phase → industry verticals.
- **Standout:** Solutions organized by *outcome*, Platform by *operational phase*. Two different cuts of the same product.
- **Keywords:** automate, integrate, analytics, HACCP, multi-site, AI Native.

### Supy (MENA) — multi-branch operating system
- **Nav:** Product · Solutions (by use case + by type) · Resources (incl. Platform Comparison) · Company · Pricing.
- **Homepage arc:** Hero ("operating system for multi-branch") → social proof (3000+, 4.9/5) → 3 product pillars → value props → **results (25% food cost reduction)** → segment grid → 6 customer stories → support → integrations → podcast → final CTA.
- **Standout:** Quantified results up high · segment grid · podcast for authority · "integrations that actually work."
- **Keywords:** operating system, multi-branch, cut costs, COGS visibility, variance, eliminate spreadsheets.

### Tenzo (UK) — the intelligence layer (best B2B structure)
- **Nav:** Product (Capabilities / Use Cases / Integrations) · Solutions (by business size + **by role**) · Resources · Company.
- **Homepage arc:** Hero ("one place for all your data") → social proof (TrustScore + logos) → 6 capabilities → feature deep-dives → demo video → **quantified testimonials** → integrations (90+) → **competitor comparison table** → **role matrix** → **FAQ objection handling** → final CTA.
- **Standout:** On-page comparison table (vs Traditional BI / POS-native / all-in-one / LLMs) · by-role audience cards · FAQ. Strongest B2B narrative of the five.
- **Keywords:** one place, complete visibility, automate, from surviving to thriving, keep your best-of-breed stack.

### MarginEdge (US) — clarity + the invoice-automation wedge ⭐
- **Nav:** Solutions (Invoice Processing, Bill Payments, Cost Management, Inventory & Ordering, Recipes & Menu Analysis, AP Automation) · How It Works · Pricing · About · Resources.
- **Homepage arc:** Hero (**"Daily P&L. No data entry."**) → social proof (11,000+ clients) → **stats (5M+ invoices, 97%)** → carousel incl. **transparent pricing ($350–500/mo)** → 5 feature blocks → testimonials → partners → brand value props → final CTA.
- **Standout:** Brutally clear hero, transparent pricing on the homepage, "for restaurant people, by restaurant people" voice, proof-by-volume.
- **Keywords:** automated invoice processing, no data entry, daily P&L, know your food costs before the period ends. **This is Stockifi's wedge — they own the messaging; we must claim it for EU.**

### FoodNotify (AT/DACH) — EU, sustainability + app-led
- **Nav:** Platform (incl. Food Costing, AI-Optimization) · Solutions (by industry incl. Hotels, Canteens, Ghost Kitchen, Franchiser) · Pricing · Resources.
- **Homepage arc:** Hero ("Sustainable Restaurant Success Through Data") → logo proof → unified-platform intro → 3 feature beats (control / app / fight waste) → module grid → **sustainability stats** → case studies → testimonials → blog → resource spotlight → newsletter → final CTA.
- **Standout:** Food-waste/sustainability angle, mobile-app emphasis, broad EU industry segmentation.
- **Keywords:** all in one, standardized processes, food waste, real-time, digitalize.

## Cross-analysis

### The consensus homepage arc (all five basically follow this)
1. **Hero** — one sharp promise (outcome or differentiator)
2. **Social proof immediately** — logos + rating + scale stat
3. **Product overview** — 3–6 capability pillars
4. **Feature deep-dives** — one section per capability, each with an outcome headline
5. **Quantified results** — testimonials with hard metrics
6. **Segment/role relevance** — by restaurant type and/or role
7. **Integrations** — "works with your stack"
8. **(Stronger sites) comparison / objection handling** — table or FAQ
9. **Final CTA** — restate promise + scale
10. **Footer** — full nav + resources + trust

### Common page set (build these)
Product/feature pages (grouped) · Solutions by segment (+ optionally by role) · Resources (blog, customer stories, tools/calculators, comparisons, podcast/webinars) · Pricing · Company (about, careers, contact) · Integrations directory · Comparison pages · Demo + Login.

### Standout patterns worth stealing
| Pattern | From | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Dead-clear outcome hero | MarginEdge | "Daily P&L. No data entry." sets the bar for clarity |
| Quantified results section | Supy, Tenzo | Metrics beat adjectives |
| On-page comparison table | Tenzo | Captures "vs X" intent on the page itself |
| By-role audience cards | Tenzo | Speaks to CEO/Ops/Finance separately |
| FAQ objection handling | Tenzo | Closes B2B doubt before the demo |
| Transparent pricing teaser | MarginEdge | Builds trust, filters leads |
| Two cuts of the product (outcome + capability) | Apicbase | Serves browsers and evaluators |
| Segment grid | Supy, FoodNotify | Self-selection by operator type |

## Recommended structure for Stockifi (proposal)

### Homepage arc
1. **Hero** — lead on the wedge + outcome. Candidate angle: automated invoice processing → margins you can see. (MarginEdge owns "daily P&L"; our EU + accounting-integration + supplier-price-tracking framing differentiates.)
2. **Social proof** — 500+ restaurants, known clients (SkiGeilo, Eik&Friends, Schnitzelei…), 2–3% COGS improvement.
3. **The €3,000 / 30-day guarantee** — none of the five lead with a risk-reversal guarantee. This is a Stockifi-only differentiator. Surface it early.
4. **Product pillars** — Recipe Costing · Invoice & Supplier Price Tracking · POS & Consumption · Inventory & Variance.
5. **Feature deep-dives** — one section each, outcome headlines.
6. **Quantified results / testimonials** — pull from customer stories.
7. **Segments** — independent · groups (multi-location) · hotels with F&B · bars.
8. **Integrations** — lead with the 69-integration breadth (a real strength).
9. **Comparison or FAQ** — Tenzo-style, once comparison pages exist.
10. **Final CTA + footer.**

### Differentiators to weave throughout
Automated invoice processing + **EU/Nordic accounting + POS integrations** · supplier price tracking depth · the **risk-free guarantee** · food-cost focus (not a bloated all-in-one) · does not replace POS/accounting.
