# Image generation prompts — Solutions page + CTA footer

Generated for ElevenLabs (also works in Seedream / Nano Banana 2). Follows the canonical thumbnail aesthetic spec (`feedback_thumbnail_editorial_direction.md`): 10-point documentary DNA, 10-step construction order, no named photographers/directors/films, film stocks allowed, no readable text, no em dashes.

- **Lighting:** natural daylight only (golden hour is fine). No evening, dusk, or tungsten-dominant scenes. Warmth comes from daylight plus wood, brass, copper and brick, not from artificial interior light.
- **Solutions cards:** aspect ratio 4:3, composed for a safe square crop (square on desktop, landscape on mobile).
- **CTA footer:** ultra-wide panoramic 21:9, cycling background.
- **Faces:** where a person appears, genuine warm/positive expression, caught mid-moment, never posing for or facing the camera.
