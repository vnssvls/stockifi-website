# Blueprint — Product page template

> One layout for all 4 capability pages. Content swaps per page; sections + intent stay the same.
> Pages: Recipe Costing `/product/recipe-costing` · Supplier Price Tracking `/product/supplier-price-tracking` · POS & Consumption `/product/pos-integration` · Inventory & Variance `/product/inventory-variance`
> Type: static · **Audience:** operator evaluating a specific capability · **Goal/CTA:** Book a demo (→ `/contact`).

## Per-page primary keyword
| Page | Primary kw |
|---|---|
| Recipe Costing | recipe costing software |
| Supplier Price Tracking | supplier price tracking (secondary: automated invoice processing) |
| POS & Consumption | theoretical vs actual food cost software |
| Inventory & Variance | restaurant inventory management software |

## Sections
### 1. Hero — feature promise
- **Blocks:** H1 (the outcome this feature delivers) · subhead · CTA "Book a demo" · feature visual (relevant screen).
- **Copy intent:** the specific margin/control outcome of THIS capability, not the whole product.

### 2. The problem — pain it removes
- **Blocks:** short framing of the manual/blind-spot pain this feature kills (e.g. supplier prices creeping up unnoticed).
- **Copy intent:** name the pain plainly; sets up the fix.

### 3. How it works — mechanism
- **Blocks:** 2-4 steps or annotated screens showing the feature in action.
- **Copy intent:** recipes/ingredients framing where relevant; concrete, not abstract.

### 4. Benefits — outcomes
- **Blocks:** 3 outcome points (what they gain), each = title + one line.

### 5. Proof — relevant testimonial / metric
- **Blocks:** a quote or number tied to this capability · link to the customer story.

### 6. Fits-the-system — cross-link
- **Blocks:** one line on how this connects to the other 3 capabilities (the 4-step) · links to the other product pages. POS & Integrations pages also link to `/integrations`.

### 7. CTA band
- **Blocks:** restate outcome · "Book a demo". Optional slim risk-reversal line (guarantee) near CTA, keep small.

### 8. FAQ (optional, 3-4)
- Feature-specific objections.

## Metadata (per page)
Title 50–60 + meta description ~160 built on the page's primary keyword (finalize in SEO step).

## Internal links out
Other 3 product pages · relevant solution pages · a customer story · /pricing · /contact. (POS/Integrations → /integrations.)
